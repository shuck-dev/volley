# Fern

The last champ before the protagonist. The dead friend, rendered into Construction as the championship duel.

---

## Who they are

A close friend of the protagonist. Their death is the source of the protagonist's grief.

---

## Construction

Fern is the world volley champion.

---

## The doubled meaning

"Becoming the champ" is winning the title and reaching the dead friend, in one motion. The protagonist does not separate them. Both pulls satisfy at the same beat at the championship duel, and neither was the thing the protagonist actually needed.
