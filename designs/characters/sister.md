# Stephanie

Zach's younger sister. The tinkerer in Construction; at the counter of the family shop in Reality.

The art bible at [`../art/bible.md`](../art/bible.md) § 4 names the body and the visual role; this profile holds the relationship and the arc.

---

## Who they are

Less weighted by the death than Zach. The protagonist can sit with her; she is willing to do the work he cannot. She is the bridge.

In Construction she rests, procrastinates, works at her own pace; finished items land on a done tray with a soft chime. Different rhythm from Zach, different relationship to the protagonist, same family ease. She talks less than he does, hands things back when they are done.

In Reality she is at the counter of the family shop, after Zach goes missing.

---

## Across the arc

**Construction** (Part 1). The tinkerer behind the workshop bench in the garden. Takes a corner of Zach's stall as her workshop and upgrades whatever the protagonist brings her.

**The break.** Stephanie is at the counter of the family shop, crying. Zach is gone. No one has heard from him.

**Reconstruction** (Part 2). Reachable in Reality at the family shop. The protagonist's source of family-side context as the search for Zach unfolds.

**The cliff and the gate.** Off-screen.

**Postgame.** Still there.
