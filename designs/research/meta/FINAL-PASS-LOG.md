### F1. Section 10 close: Active Break C, end on a particular not abstraction.

Current (final paragraph of section 10):
> Open at every layer that touches its players, closed at the layers that touch its money. The strategy scales. The openness has limits, and those limits are what the company keeps.

Proposed: drop the closing summary and end the section on the Steam Hardware Survey number ("...reached 5.33% in March 2026, the highest figure ever recorded"), or on the "blank" password from the section's opening, recalling the inclusio.

Rationale: the Persuasion Audit (Active Break C) calls for the section to end on its loaded particular rather than three sentences of synthesis. The current close is also a paragraph that summarises rather than lands; STYLE.md's "no paragraph summarises the previous paragraph" cuts against it. Deferred because the existing close does carry a clean structural claim ("open at every layer that touches its players, closed at the layers that touch its money") which is one of the essay's most-quotable lines, and removing it loses a load-bearing summary.

### F2. Section 13 close: Active Break D, drop the generic kicker.

Current (final two sentences):
> Openness is a practice, not a guarantee. The practice still beats the alternative.

Rationale: "alternative" is a Latinate end-word that drifts; the kicker is generic where the section's loaded sentence (West's plain-spoken line) sits one paragraph above.
