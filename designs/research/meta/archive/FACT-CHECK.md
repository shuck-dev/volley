### CONTRADICTED claims

**1. GDC State of the Industry 2025 layoff stats: "One in ten developers had been laid off in the past year; one in four in the past two."**

- Essay says: 10% laid off past year; 25% past two years.
- Independent sources say: GDC 2025 reported 11% laid off in the past year (close enough), but the "past two years" figure most widely reported by GDC's own writeup, Game Developer, PC Gamer, and Variety is **28% globally / 33% in the US** over two years, not 25%. The "one in four" framing under-rounds. The 2026 GDC follow-up report cited the same ~33% US figure.
- Sources:
  - https://www.gamedeveloper.com/business/survey-one-in-four-developers-laid-off-over-the-past-two-years
  - https://www.videogameschronicle.com/news/more-than-a-quarter-of-devs-surveyed-by-gdc-were-laid-off-in-the-past-two-years-and-half-of-them-dont-have-a-new-job/
- Recommended fix: Change "one in four in the past two" to "more than one in four (28%) in the past two, and one in three among US developers" or simply "more than one in four".

**2. Garry's Mod: "approached Newman to ship it commercially on 29 November 2006, reportedly handing him the full Source engine code in exchange for a 50% revenue share, and the game has sold over 20 million copies."**

- Essay says: 20 million copies.
- Independent sources say: 20M was reached by September 2021. As of November 2024 (18th anniversary), the figure is 25.5 million. The essay's "over 20 million" is technically true but stale by ~5M.
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Garry's_Mod
- Recommended fix: "has sold over 25 million copies" (or "over 20 million", but the larger figure is more accurate and equally well-sourced).

- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Outreachy
