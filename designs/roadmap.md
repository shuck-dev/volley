# Roadmap

Volley! ships in five phases. Each name below is the state the game is in at the end of that phase. The real plan lives on Linear, in the cycles and projects under each phase; this page is the long view.

## Prototype

A public itch.io demo with the core loop tight, first-pass art and sound throughout, and the repository open on GitHub.

## Alpha

Part 1 playable end to end: real art landed, the cast and the tournament filled out, and the break designed.

## Beta

The whole arc playable from start to finish: the break, Part 2, and the cliff and the call, with every style holding up across the run.

## v1

The Steam release, polished to the platform's bar and ready to ship.

## Post-release

The long tail: more cast and content, the Regret postgame, Mac, multiplayer.
